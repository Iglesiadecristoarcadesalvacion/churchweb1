About Us

Welcome to Iglesia de Cristo Arca De Salvacion, a vibrant and diverse church community committed to serving and worshiping together in the name of Christ. Our church is not just a place of worship; it's a family where you belong.

Our Mission

At Arca De Salvacion, our mission is to bring people closer to God and to each other. We are dedicated to sharing the love and teachings of Jesus Christ with our community, guiding individuals towards a deeper relationship with God, and fostering a sense of unity and belonging among our members.

Our Values

  • Faith: We believe in the power of faith and the transformation it brings to our lives.
  • Community: We are a tight-knit community that supports and cares for one another.
  • Service: We are called to serve, both within our church and in the broader community.
  • Love: Love is at the core of everything we do, following the example of Jesus.

Ministries

Arca De Salvacion offers various ministries catering to different age groups and needs. From children's programs to youth groups, Bible studies to community outreach, there are opportunities for everyone to get involved and grow spiritually.

Get Involved

We invite you to become a part of our loving community. Whether you're looking for a church home or simply seeking answers to life's questions, we're here for you. Join us for a service, connect with our members, and discover the joy of a life devoted to Christ.
